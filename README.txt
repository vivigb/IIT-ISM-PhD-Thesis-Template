
#PhD Thesis Template for IIT (ISM), Dhanbad
Welcome to the PhD Thesis Template repository for IIT (ISM), Dhanbad, India. This template is designed to help you create a professional and polished PhD thesis using LaTeX. It is prepared in Overleaf and has also been successfully tested in the TeX Live environment.

Features
Structured Template: Predefined sections for your thesis, including title page, abstract, acknowledgments, table of contents, chapters, references, and appendices.
Consistent Formatting: Ensures consistent formatting throughout the document with predefined styles for headings, subheadings, figures, tables, and references.
Bibliography Support: Easily manage your references with BibTeX.
Customizable: Modify the template to suit your specific needs and preferences.
